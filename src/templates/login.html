CONSIDER
A novel approach to Collaborative Learning

Teachers

Instructors can create courses and sections, add students, organize them into groups, set up questions and discussion rounds, toggle features such as anonymity and discussion structure, and download the transcripts of their class' discussion.

Students

Students can answer the questions, comment on their peers' answers and discussion posts, and download the transcripts of their own group's discussion. Click below for a step-by-step tutorial about how to use this app.

Download

Educators can download the code and launch their own instance of CONSIDER on the Google App Engine server, and then use it in their own courses. That way, the student information will stay with their organization alone.
